    Frequently Asked Questions

    Find answers to common questions about our products and services.

    What materials do you use for your products?

    We use high-quality aluminium and tempered glass for our windows, doors, and frames. These materials are chosen for their durability, energy efficiency, and modern aesthetic, ensuring long-lasting performance in Botswana's climate.

    Do you offer installation services?

    Yes, we provide professional installation services. Our skilled team ensures that your windows and doors are fitted with precision, adhering to industry standards for safety and performance.

    How can I place an order?

    Browse our product catalog on the website, add items to your cart, and contact us via phone (+267 75 062 345) or email (Cmaphika@gmail.com) to finalize your order. We’ll confirm availability, arrange payment in Botswana Pula, and schedule delivery or installation.

    What is the warranty on your products?

    We offer a 5-year warranty on all our aluminium windows, doors, and frames, covering manufacturing defects and issues related to professional installation. Contact us for details on warranty claims.

    Can you create custom designs?

    Absolutely! We specialize in custom window and door designs tailored to your specifications. Whether you need unique sizes, shapes, or finishes, our team will work with you to bring your vision to life. Contact us to discuss your project.
