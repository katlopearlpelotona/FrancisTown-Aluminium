    Our Story

    Founded over a decade ago in Francistown, Botswana, FrancisTown Aluminium has grown from a small supplier to a trusted name in aluminium windows, doors, and building materials. Our dedication to craftsmanship and customer satisfaction drives us to deliver innovative solutions for homes and businesses across the region.

    Our Values

    Quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction are at the core of everything we do. We strive to exceed expectations by using premium materials, maintaining rigorous standards, and fostering strong relationships with our clients.

    Why Choose Us

    With years of experience, a skilled team, and a commitment to excellence, we offer unmatched expertise in aluminium products and services. Our personalized approach ensures every project meets your unique needs, backed by a 5-year warranty.
